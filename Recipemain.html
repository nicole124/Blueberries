      • Pasta al Rustica

    INGREDIENTS

      200g Spaghetti
      350g Sweet cherry tomatoes
      75ml Single cream
      100g Bacon

    HOW TO :

    1. Cook the pasta according to intructions
    2. Fry the tomatoes over medium heat, and add the bacon. Fry for 2 minutues
    3. Add the wild garlic and rosemary paste, mix well and cook for 2 minutes
    4. Stir in the rustica paste
    5. Add the red pepper spice to the pan. Pour in the cream and stir well - letting it gentle bubble for 2-3 minutes
    6. Drain pasta and serve it with some parmesan on top

      Jerk Chicken

    INGREDIENTS

      4 Skinless chicken thigh fillets
      120g Basmati rice
      200ml Coconut milk
      210 Tinned kidney beans

    HOW TO :

    1. Heat the oven to 200C and boil 150ml water in the kettle
    2. Mix jerk seasoning and the jerk paste together in a mixing bowl with a drop of oil
    3. Add the chicken thighs and mix around well to coat
    4. Place the chiken on a baking tray and cook for 20 minutes
    5. Put 100ml boiling water into a sacepan with chicken stock and mix well. Add the coconut milk in here too
    6. Add the rice and kidney beans to this, along with all the remaining juices. Mix well gently, and let it simmer for 15 minutes
    7. Serve the rice with the chcken and sweetcorn, and squeeze the lime on top for extra seasoning

      Spanish Paella

    INGREDIENTS

      150g King prawns
      50g Chorizo
      1 Onion
      150g Paella rice
      1 Red pepper
      230g Tin chopped tomatoes
      2 chicken thighs

    HOW TO :

    1. Heat some oil in a wide frying pan over medium-high heat and fry the chicken until golden.
    2. Add the onion, pepper, chorizo and paella garnish and fry for 2 minutes
    3. Pour in the chopped tomatoes, mix well and fry for 2 more mins.
    4. Boil a full kettle of water. Add the rice, a splash of white wine and the saffron seasoning and stir well
    5. Stir in the paella paste, then add 600ml of boiling water and mix well. Bring it back to the boil and then reduce the heat to medium-low and leave to cook for 15 mins (no lid).
    6. Stir in the prawns and peas into the rice anServe the rice with the chcken and sweetcord cook for 5 more minutes
    7. Squeeze some lemon juice over the paella, and serve with extra lemon wedges

      Creamy honey mustard chicken

      2 Chicken Breasts
      500g Potatoes
      1 Small Onion
      200g Button mushrooms
      100ml Creme fraiche
      100ml Cornflour
      100ml Honey
      100ml Butter

    HOW TO :

    1. Heat a little oil in a pan and fry the chicken until golden, then remove and keep aside.
    2. Boil the potatoes until tender, and begin the next step meanwhile
    3. Put a full kettle on to boil. Then in the same pan, fry the onion in a little oil for 3 mins.
    4. Turn the heat up and add the mushrooms.
    5. Fry for 2 mins then stir in the white wine reduction, and add the supreme sauce stock
    6. Add the supreme sauce stock with 250ml boiling water. Mix well and cook for 5 mins.
    7. Mix 1 tsp cornflour with 2 tsps water in a cup, then add to the pan. Bring to the boil, then simmer for 2 mins.
    8. Stir in the crème fraîche then return the chicken (and juices) to the pan and simmer until cooked through, and then stir in 1-2 tsp of honey.
    9. Mash the potatoes with a large knob of butter, then stir the herb mash mix through it.
    10. Serve the chicken and sauce over mash
